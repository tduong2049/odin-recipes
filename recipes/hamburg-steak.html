Hamburg Steak

Description

Hamburg steak is a patty of ground beef that originated in Germany. It was made popular worldwide by migrating Germans and continues to be a stable in Japanese and American Southern cuisine.

Many variations of hamburg steak exists now. Some recipes may include gravy, carmelized onions, or Japanese mayo. For now, we'll be keeping it simple with a recipe for just the patty.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Egg
  • Bread crumbs
  • Onions
  • Milk
  • Cooking oil

Steps

  1. Chop up onions.
  2. Mix ground beef, egg, bread crumbs, onions, and milk in a bowl.
  3. Scoop up a ball of the mix and shape into patty using your hands.
  4. Oil the frying pan.
  5. Cook the patties on the frying pan.
